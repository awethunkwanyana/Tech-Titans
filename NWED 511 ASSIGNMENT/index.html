Why Education Matters in ICT

The ICT sector changes rapidly. Staying current with formal education and self-learning ensures you remain competitive and employable in a high-demand industry.

  • University degrees open doors to senior roles
  • Certifications prove hands-on technical skills
  • Online courses keep your knowledge fresh and current
  • Practical projects complement any qualification
